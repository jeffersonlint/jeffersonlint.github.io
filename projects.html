Unibloom

A scheduling and academic preparedness application developed for Android for college students. The app learns user preferences and behaviors and makes suggestions of study times that work best for the individual user.

  • ~ Frontend development done using Flutter

  • ~ Backend API development done primarily using Kotlin

  • ~ Designed and developed in a team of 5 over the course of a year

Embedded System: Hand Sanitizer Module

In light of COVID-19, the topical choice for an embedded system is one that helps people stay safe and sanitized. The entire project was completed from the ground up; materials for the case of the device were 3D printed, a custom PCB was designed and ordered, and both hardware and software components were designed from scratch.

  • ~ Software modules were organized in C, with contributions from ValvanoWare

  • ~ Applications of motor control, motion-detection sensors, and liquid-level sensors

  • ~ Designed and developed in a team of 4 over the course of three weeks

Digital System Design Work
